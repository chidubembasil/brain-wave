Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4 hours.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your full name, contact information, and a detailed description of your question or issue. Screenshots are helpful for technical problems.

Yes, we offer phone support during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM, Monday-Friday). You can call us at +234 706 043 6659.

Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a personalized demo of our tutoring platform. We'll show you how Brainwave's expert educators can help improve your academic performance.
