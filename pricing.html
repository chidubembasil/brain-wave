Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing plans

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfers, debit cards, and mobile money payments.

Is there a free bootcamp?

Yes, all new users get a 7-day free bootcamp - a training and test program to assess your knowledge and ability on different subjects before choosing a plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Once you stop your subscription, your access will be cut off immediately.
